## Who I Am

Welcome to my personal site — a place for writing about interesting technologies I've worked on professionally or simply taken an interest in.

I'm a firmware and wireless engineer with deep roots in 802.11. I hold CWNE #570 — one of roughly 500 active Certified Wireless Network Experts worldwide — and have spent my career at the intersection of Wi-Fi hardware and the software that drives it. Both Wi-Fi and I were ratified in 1997.

Ever since I was young and obsessively played the first Halo: Combat Evolved on my Dad's work desktop, I've been obsessed with computers. I studied Computer Science at UNC Chapel Hill, graduating in 2020 with a minor in Hispanic Studies. My first role in wireless was as a part-time wireless administrator working under the wireless architect at UNC Chapel Hill, performing site surveys across campus.

## Professional Background

My career has spanned Wi-Fi 6/6E/7 firmware validation at Cisco Meraki, automated RF test infrastructure, and hands-on debugging in shielded chambers with spectrum analyzers and protocol captures. I've validated access point firmware across multiple chipset platforms and built test suites that catch regressions before they ship.

Professionally I aspire to have a hand in the development of embedded firmware systems and more intimately become familiar with the implementation of the protocols I've studied.

## What I'm Building

I'm focused on embedded firmware development in C++ — writing the code that runs directly on microcontrollers and wireless SoCs. Current projects include:

- **ESP32 Wi-Fi Scanner & Channel Analyzer** — A handheld 2.4 GHz spectrum tool built on the ESP32-S3 with real-time OLED visualization. Overlap-aware congestion scoring, signal-weighted interference modeling, and clean C++ architecture. [View on GitHub](https://github.com/Vulfid/arduinoWiFiAnalyzer)

## Technical Focus

- **Languages:** C++ (embedded/firmware), Python (test automation), C
- **Platforms:** ESP32-S3, ARM Cortex-M, PlatformIO, Arduino framework
- **Wireless:** 802.11ax/be (Wi-Fi 6/7), RF analysis, channel planning, roaming (802.11r/k/v)
- **Tools:** PlatformIO, Wireshark, Ekahau, shielded chamber environments
- **Certifications:** CWNE #570, CWDP, CWAP, CWSP, CWNA
